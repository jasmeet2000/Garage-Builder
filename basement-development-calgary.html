Premium Custom Basement Renovations

Your basement represents nearly half of your home's total square footage. Leaving it unfinished is a massive waste of valuable living space. Developing your basement not only creates room for family growth, home offices, and recreational areas, but it also yields a major boost in home equity and resale values.

At PeakCity, we provide a complete, stress-free basement development service. We specialize in custom layouts, framing, high-output insulation (making cold concrete floors warm), electrical routing, plumbing rough-ins for bathrooms/wet bars, legal bedroom additions featuring egress windows, and legal secondary rental suites.

Legal Egress Bedrooms

Safety is our top priority. We cut and excavate concrete walls to install legal, code-compliant egress windows, ensuring safe escape routes and natural sunlight.

Entertainment & Media Lounges

We build luxury home theaters, wet bars, recreational game rooms, gym spaces, and quiet, soundproof home offices tailored to your lifestyle.

Our Turnkey Basement Development Process

1. Design & Layout

We draft detailed architectural blueprints, planning electrical layouts, bathroom configurations, storage spaces, and plumbing lines to maximize usable square footage.

2. Premium Moisture Defense

Basements sit below ground. We apply high-performance sub-flooring moisture barriers, insulated framing, and vapor seals to keep the space dry, warm, and mold-free.

3. Legal Permit Compliance

We secure all City of Calgary building, trade, plumbing, and electrical permits, organizing inspections at every stage to ensure 100% legal compliance.
