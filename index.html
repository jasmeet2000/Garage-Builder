Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Calgary homeowners want to know before starting a garage project

How much does it cost to build a garage in Calgary?
+

A single car garage in Calgary typically costs $25,000–$35,000. A double car garage runs $40,000–$60,000, and a triple car garage ranges from $65,000–$90,000. Prices vary based on size, finishes, and foundation type.

Do I need a permit to build a garage in Calgary?
+

Yes, a building permit is required for any new garage construction in Calgary. PeakCity Garages handles all City of Calgary permit applications on your behalf.

How long does it take to build a garage in Calgary?
+

Most garage builds take 2–4 weeks once permits are approved. The City of Calgary permit process typically takes 4–8 weeks. Total project timeline is usually 8–12 weeks from first consultation.

Do you build garages in winter in Calgary?
+

Yes! PeakCity Garages builds year-round in Calgary. We use winter construction best practices to ensure your concrete and framing meet all quality standards regardless of temperature.

What areas near Calgary do you serve?
+

We serve Calgary and surrounding communities including Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, Okotoks, Strathmore, High River, Langdon, and Crossfield.
