Our Story as a Calgary Garage Contractor

PeakCity Garages was founded with a single goal: to give Calgary homeowners a better way to build. After years in general residential construction, our founders realized that many companies treated garages as an afterthought. We decided to make them our primary focus.

By specializing in garages, we've refined our processes, built stronger relationships with local suppliers, and developed a deep understanding of Calgary's unique zoning and climatic requirements.

Today, we are proud to be one of the top-rated garage builders in Calgary, known for our integrity, craftsmanship, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Over 500 garages built across Calgary, Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks.
