Frequently Asked Questions About Garage Costs in Calgary

Real answers from Calgary's most trusted garage builders.

How much does a double garage cost to build in Calgary in 2026?

A standard double-car detached garage (22×22 to 24×24 ft) in Calgary typically costs between $45,000 and $70,000 in 2026. This includes the concrete slab, framing, roofing, standard vinyl siding, two overhead doors, man door, and basic electrical rough-in. Adding insulation, a 200-amp panel, and heated floors can bring it to $65,000–$85,000.

Does the estimate include permits?

The calculator estimates do not include permit fees, which vary by municipality and project size. In Calgary, permit fees typically range from $800 to $2,500 for a detached garage. PeakCity Garages handles all permit applications on your behalf — we include permit coordination in all our full-service packages.

What's the biggest factor that affects garage cost?

Size is the #1 cost driver, followed by electrical requirements and insulation level. A 24×24 double garage with no upgrades costs significantly less than the same footprint with a heated concrete floor, 200-amp panel, and premium siding. Our calculator accounts for all of these variables to give you a realistic range.

Can I build a garage in the winter in Calgary?

Yes! PeakCity Garages builds year-round in Calgary, including winter months. We use heated enclosures for concrete pours and adjust our schedule around extreme cold snaps (−25°C or colder). Winter builds can sometimes be advantageous — contractors are less busy and lead times can be shorter.

How accurate is this calculator?

Our calculator is based on real 2026 Calgary market pricing from our completed projects. It's designed to give you a reliable ballpark within 10–20% of the actual quote. Site-specific factors like lot access, soil conditions, and existing structures can affect final pricing. Request a free on-site quote for your exact number.
