Premium Concrete Garage Pads Built to Last

The foundation is the most critical element of your garage. In Calgary, our expansive clay soils and dramatic temperature swings (the freeze-thaw cycles) put enormous stress on concrete. A poorly poured pad will quickly crack, heave, and compromise the wooden frame of your garage.

At PeakCity, we utilize engineered foundation blueprints that meet or exceed all Alberta Building Code requirements. We construct reinforced pads featuring thickened edges, engineered gravel packing, and heavy steel rebar grids to ensure maximum strength and durability.

High-Strength Concrete Mix

We pour only premium 32 MPa concrete featuring air entrainment, designed specifically to resist scaling caused by winter road salt and freeze-thaw cracking.

Rebar Reinforcement Grid

We do not use cheap wire mesh. Our crews build a solid grid using thick steel reinforcing bars (rebar) to withstand heavy vehicles and trucks.

Our Complete Concrete Work Scope

Excavation & Grading

We remove existing sod and soil, establishing the correct grading layout to direct surface water away from the garage and home foundations.

Sub-Base Compaction

We lay down a minimum of 4-6 inches of crushed gravel, thoroughly compacting it with heavy plate packers to establish a uniform, non-settling sub-base.

Professional Finishing

Our crew cuts relief joints to control natural concrete expansion and applies a light broom finish for a clean, non-slip vehicle surface.
