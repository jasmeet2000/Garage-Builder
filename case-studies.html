Custom Double Detached Garage — Evanston NW

This homeowner needed a secure, winter-ready double car garage to protect their SUVs from Calgary's harsh winter weather. The site was an empty, sloped clay lot at the rear of the property. We graded the slope, laid an engineered reinforced concrete slab with steel rebar, framed 2x6 exterior walls, matched the home's vinyl siding and asphalt shingles, and installed a premium insulated double overhead door.

Location Evanston, Calgary NW
Project Scope 22' × 22' Detached
Total Timeline 18 Days (On-Site)
Value Added +$55,000 Equity

Luxury Triple Garage & Mechanical Workshop — Mahogany SE

The client requested a massive multi-car garage that could double as a heated mechanical workshop and store their classic car. We engineered a massive foundation featuring thickened edges to support a car lift, built R-20 insulated walls with complete vapor barriers, installed a 60A electrical sub-panel, mounted overhead LED strip panels, and set up a gas-fired unit heater system.

Location Mahogany, Calgary SE
Project Scope 32' × 22' Custom Shop
Total Timeline 24 Days (On-Site)
Features 60A Panel, Gas Heat
