The Benefits of a Detached Garage in Calgary

In Calgary, detached garages are incredibly popular, especially in newer communities with laneway access. They offer several advantages over attached garages:

  • ✓Noise Control — Keep power tool noise and vehicle sounds away from the main living areas.
  • ✓Flexibility — Easier to expand or add a secondary suite (laneway house) if zoning allows.
  • ✓Safety — Combustible materials and fumes are stored away from your primary residence.
  • ✓Space Optimization — A dedicated area for hobbyists, workshops, or large vehicle storage.

Detached Garage FAQs

Common questions from Calgary homeowners about detached garages

What is the most common detached garage size in Calgary?
+

The most popular size is 20' × 22' or 22' × 22'. This comfortably fits two vehicles and allows for basic storage along the side walls. Many homeowners opt for 22' × 24' to accommodate larger SUVs and trucks.

Can I add a secondary suite above my detached garage in Calgary?
+

Yes — many Calgary communities are now zoned for laneway houses or garden suites above garages. This requires specific permits and structural modifications designed from the start. We can design your garage with this future possibility built in.

Do detached garages need a separate utility connection?
+

Usually, electrical is pulled from the main house panel via a buried conduit. If you want gas heating or water service, separate utility connections or a sub-panel will be needed — we'll specify all of this in your quote.
