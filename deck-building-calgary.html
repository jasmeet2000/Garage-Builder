Premium Custom Backyard Deck Construction

A custom-built deck is the ultimate way to extend your home's living space outdoors, creating the perfect environment for summer barbecues, family gatherings, and relaxing in the sun. Given Calgary's seasonal weather extremes, building a deck that is beautiful, safe, and durable requires premium materials and masterful structural framing.

At PeakCity, our licensed carpenters handle everything from initial design layouts and securing City of Calgary building permits to building solid structural supports (helical piles or concrete footings), installing premium decking boards, custom glass or aluminum railings, privacy walls, and pergolas.

Pressure-Treated & Cedar Wood

We build beautiful, highly durable natural wood decks utilizing premium pressure-treated lumber or gorgeous, moisture-resistant Western Red Cedar.

Low-Maintenance Composite Decks

We specialize in premium composite decking systems (Trex, TimberTech, AZEK). They resist fading, rotting, and scratching—completely eliminating the need to stain or paint.

Our Structural Deck Promise

Helical Screw Piles

To prevent deck sinking or frost shifting, we install heavy steel helical screw piles deep below the frost line (minimum 7-8 feet) for unbeatable stability.

Custom Architectural Design

We design multi-level deck layouts, integrated stairs, bench seating, privacy screens, and lighting schemes to fit your home's unique layout.

Licensed Journeyman Crew

Our decks are built by licensed journeyman carpenters. We do not cut corners, ensuring all joists, beams, and hangers meet structural building codes.
