Privacy Policy

How PeakCity Garages collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Home › Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026  |  Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

PeakCity Garages Calgary ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at www.peakcitygarages.ca or contact us to inquire about our garage construction services.

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the site.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out a quote request form, contact form, or call us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property address or location
  • Project details (garage type, size, features requested)
  • Any message or notes you include in your submission

2b. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (where you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit

2c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. These may include:

  • Google Analytics — to understand website traffic and user behaviour
  • Google Ads Tags — to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Facebook Pixel — to serve relevant ads and track conversions
  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your quote requests, inquiries, and messages
  • To provide you with estimates and information about our garage construction services
  • To schedule on-site consultations and project visits
  • To send follow-up communications related to your inquiry
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To analyze how visitors use our website (via Google Analytics)
  • To show you relevant advertisements on Google and Facebook
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • EmailJS — our contact form processing service, used solely to deliver your message to us
  • Google LLC — for analytics and advertising services (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook) — for advertising and retargeting (governed by Meta's Privacy Policy)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and customer inquiry data for up to 3 years from the date of submission, or as long as necessary to complete a project or resolve any related disputes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Privacy Rights (PIPEDA — Canada)

As a Canadian business, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdraw consent to the collection and use of your information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data transmitted between your browser and our server.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Google Maps, social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated policy on this page with the revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
